About JomDonate

Learn More About Our Mission

JomDonate is a community-driven platform dedicated to helping those in need. Our mission is to empower individuals, support local projects, and make meaningful impact across the country.

  • Over 5,000 people helped
  • 120 community projects completed
  • Thousands of volunteers engaged nationwide

Our team works tirelessly to connect donors, volunteers, and communities. Every contribution, big or small, creates lasting change.

Why Choose JomDonate

JomDonate helps communities reduce waste and increase impact by making donations smarter and faster. Whether it's surplus food from businesses or items from the public, our platform uses AI + location matching to route donations to the right NGO based on acceptance rules, urgency, and distance.

How It Works?

AI Donation Sorting

Type an item or food description and get instant category, acceptance decision, and recommended donation options based on NGO rules.

Nearest NGO Matching

Google Maps helps match donors to nearby NGOs with the best pickup route, reducing delays and improving delivery success rate.

Real-Time Alerts & Automation

Firebase + Cloud Functions automate listings, send notifications, and trigger urgent matches when food is near expiry.

Donate to Trusted Shelters

We connect donations to verified partners such as orphanages, elderly care homes, and animal shelters (dog & cat) based on what they currently need.
